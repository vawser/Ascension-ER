Supports Elden Ring version 1.10.1

[color=#f1c232][size=4][b]Summary[/b][/size][/color]
Lands Betwixt is a fleshed-out overhaul for Elden Ring, focusing on quality of life enhancements as well as additional content in the form of new items, armor, spells, etc. New features have been implemented to deepen the RPG aspects of Elden Ring.

[color=#f1c232][size=4][b]Major Changes[/b][/size][/color]
[b]Journey Configuration[/b]
Tweak how you start your playthrough, selecting between different difficulty options, starting location and other modifiers.

[b]Gladiator's Gauntlet[/b]
A PVE survival-based mini-game with configurable options, allowing it to adapted to fit every stage of progression.

[b]Boss Rebirth[/b]
The ability to resurrect previously defeated bosses.

[b]Transmogrification[/b]
The ability to visually override your head or body/arm/leg armor.

[b]Bloodforge[/b]
The ability to improve talismans via Bloodsoaked Runes, encouraging completion of side-bosses.

[b]Castigations[/b]
The option to increase the difficulty of the game in various ways, in exchange for more chances at Bloodsoaked Runes.

[b]New Affinities[/b]
Support for three new affinities: Soporific, Frenzied and Blighted.

[b]New Items[/b]
New items, spells, armor, etc such as the Fractured Blade, Shinobi's Whetstone, Vow of Silence and more to be discovered.

[color=#f1c232][size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size][/color]
To use this mod you will need to:
[list]
[*]Download Mod Engine 2: go to https://github.com/soulsmods/ModEngine2/releases
[*]Click Assets, then download the the top one, NOT the source code.
[*]Extract Mod Engine 2 somewhere onto your PC.
[*]Extract the contents of this zip file into the "mod" folder in the same directory as the "modengine2_launcher.exe".
[*]Launch Steam once so Steam Services are running.
[*]Click launchmod_eldenring.bat to launch the game.
[/list]

[color=#f1c232][size=4][b]Uninstallation[/b][/size][/color]
To remove this mod you will need to:
[list]
[*]Delete the contents of the "mod" folder in the Mod Engine 2 directory.
[/list]
